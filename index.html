About DaMaC

Our Vision

To create a world where every Ugandan community has the resources, knowledge, and support needed to thrive independently and sustainably.

Our Mission

We empower communities across Uganda through targeted education programs, sustainable development initiatives, healthcare access, and compassionate outreach, fostering self-reliance and lasting positive change.

At DaMaC, we believe in the power of community-driven initiatives that respect local cultures and traditions. Our collaborative approach involves working alongside community leaders to identify specific needs and implement tailored solutions that create sustainable impact.

Success Story

Transforming Namatala Village

In 2021, DaMaC partnered with the Namatala community to address critical water shortages affecting over 500 families. Through collaborative efforts, we implemented a comprehensive water management system that includes:

  • Construction of 3 community wells
  • Installation of rainwater harvesting systems at schools
  • Training of 12 community members as water technicians
  • Establishment of a community-led water management committee

Today, Namatala residents have reliable access to clean water, cases of waterborne diseases have decreased by 65%, and school attendance has improved as children spend less time collecting water.
